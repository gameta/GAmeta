ME 574: Kinect CSG CAD (2012)

Basic CAD operations through Microsoft Kinect. Hand gestures are used to (a) create simple sketches, (b) extrude sketches and (c) translate/rotate primitives. The primitive data is automatically fed to OpenSCAD to create the resultant 3D shape.
